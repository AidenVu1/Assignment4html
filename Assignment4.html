Identify the career you choose: Software Engineering

A brief description of the job and its responsibilities: Software Engineers design and create computer systems and applications to solve real-world problems. Software engineers, sometimes called software developers, create software for computers and applications.

The education required for such a career: You need a bachelor's degree, in computer science, computer systems engineering, software engineering or mathematics or a college degree in computer science

The future possibilities (outlook) for that career: There is a need of software engineers since there is an expected shortage over the period of 2022-2031. 44300 are expected to be employed but there are 48800 that are needed.

The salary range: $77,807-$162,772

A description why I would be interested in such a career: Even with the nuances of this job I am interested in the job and experimenting with technology. My father is a software engineer and has influenced me to learn coding.

Education Institute: University of Waterloo
